Publication

Energy-based Approach for Attack Detection in IoT Devices: A Survey

2024 | Internet of Things, Volume 27

Examines how energy consumption patterns in IoT devices can be leveraged for detecting malicious activities. With 29 billion connected IoT devices anticipated by 2030, this work synthesizes machine learning approaches for identifying attacks through power usage analysis—offering a novel dimension of IoT protection without relying on traditional signature-based methods.
