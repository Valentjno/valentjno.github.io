About

AI security leader at Accenture, focused on building resilient, secure, and compliant AI systems through red teaming and governance frameworks. PhD research in energy-based attack detection on IoT devices.

Who I Am

I'm an AI security researcher and red teamer working at the intersection of IoT security and AI on edge systems. My PhD research at the University of Catania focuses on energy-based attack detection in IoT devices and machine learning approaches for identifying malicious activity in resource-constrained systems.

I combine academic research with industry security work, conducting threat assessments and red-teaming exercises for organizations building AI systems. My approach is practical: identify risks early, document them clearly, and help teams build more secure systems from the ground up.

Your Approach

  • Threat Modeling: Structured analysis of attack surfaces and system vulnerabilities before they become problems.
  • Adversarial Testing: Red-teaming from the attacker's perspective to identify real-world risks.
  • Practical Security: Actionable recommendations, not theoretical warnings. Focus on what teams can actually implement.
